About Us

Who we are, why we exist, and how we work toward lasting change.

Our Mission

We exist to amplify the voices that matter.

AIRA was founded on the belief that policy change only sticks when it comes from the community — not just for it. We bridge the gap between lived experience and legislative change.

Our work is nonpartisan, community-first, and grounded in evidence. We believe in dialogue over division, and action over rhetoric.

Our story

How We Got Here

2017

Founded

A small group of neighbors came together after a local housing crisis. AIRA was born from that kitchen table conversation.

2019

First Policy Win

Our coalition secured the city's first renter protection ordinance after 18 months of organizing and testimony.

2021

Expanded to Education & Environment

Community feedback led us to broaden our scope. We launched two new issue campaigns with new partners.

Today

12,000+ Members & Growing

We're now a statewide network with three active campaigns and a stronger community than ever.

Advocating for a better future. Building the community that makes change happen.
